Our Story

Harp City RPC was founded with a passion for creating the most immersive and realistic emergency services roleplay experience in Ireland. We bring together dedicated individuals who value teamwork, realism, and professionalism.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a dynamic, engaging, and authentic roleplay environment where players can experience the challenges and triumphs of emergency services. We continuously innovate to keep our community at the forefront of immersive roleplay.

Our Values

  • Realism & Immersion
  • Community & Teamwork
  • Integrity & Professionalism
  • Continuous Improvement

Join us on our journey as we create a roleplay experience that's not only fun but also deeply engaging and true to life.
